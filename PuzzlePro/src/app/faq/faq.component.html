FAQ What is PuzzlePro?

This site is a plattform to play simple puzzlegames with your friends and compete to see how reaches the highest score.


Is it free to play PuzzlePro?

Yes PuzzlePro is a nonprofit project. No costs, no ads, no premium Features.


Can I still support the developers?

Yes you can! If you want to support you can reach out at our gofundme: Thank you!


How is the score calculated?

The score is 100 - the seconds you take to complete your puzzle


What happens if I manage to get the highest score of them all?

We will congratulate you by putting your name and score on our leaderboard until it gets beaten.


Can I see all of my scores?

No only your best score counts, your highscore.
